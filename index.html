About US

Rajasthan Association Germany e.V ( RAG ) There are about 2,80,000 Indians in Germany most of whom are working as professionals, Students and self-employed. Our aim is to bring ‘All the Indians living in Germany/ Germany’ together on a common platform with aim of sharing and caring for each other with our best support. Know More If you are in Germany any of your family member/ friend is on the verge of coming to Germany and need guidance/support with queries to related to Visa, Colleges, Social connects to avoid home sickness, Finding accommodation, Logistical set up, language course, Insurances, Driving licence requirements etc.
